    • Refreshly

    Refreshly is an app designed to help you maintain a healthy daily hydration routine. Track your water intake, set goals, and receive reminders to stay hydrated throughout the day.

    With a user-friendly interface, Refreshly helps you establish and reach your hydration goals effortlessly, making healthy living part of your everyday routine.

    Main Features

    Hydration Reminders

    Receive notifications throughout the day to ensure you stay hydrated.

    Track Water Intake

    Easily log the amount of water you drink and monitor your progress over time.

    Personalized Goals

    Set daily hydration goals based on your personal health needs.

    Daily Reports

    Get insights into your hydration habits and make improvements where needed.

    Privacy Policy

    Refreshly values your privacy. The app does not collect or share your personal information. All data related to your hydration habits is stored locally on your device, ensuring your privacy is maintained. No data is transmitted to third parties.

    We use local notifications to remind you to drink water, and these notifications are managed entirely within your device.

    FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

    How does Refreshly work?

    Refreshly helps you track your water intake and sends reminders throughout the day to ensure you meet your hydration goals. Simply log the amount of water you drink, and the app will keep you on track.

    Is my data secure?

    Yes, your data is completely secure. Refreshly stores all data locally on your device and does not collect or share any personal information.

    Can I customize my hydration goals?

    Absolutely! You can personalize your hydration goals based on your weight, height, and daily activity level to ensure you get the right amount of hydration.
